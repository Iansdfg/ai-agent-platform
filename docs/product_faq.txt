Q: What food is best for puppies?
A: Puppies require high-quality puppy food that is rich in protein, healthy fats, and essential nutrients. Look for food labeled specifically for puppies, as it supports their growth and development.

Q: How often should I feed my dog?
A: Puppies should be fed 3–4 times a day, while adult dogs typically require 2 meals per day. Feeding schedules may vary depending on breed and size.
