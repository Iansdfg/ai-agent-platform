# Return Policy

## Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, you may return eligible items within 30 days of delivery.

This return policy applies to all products purchased through our platform unless otherwise specified.

---

## Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, the item must meet the following conditions:

- The item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it
- The item must be in the original packaging
- You must provide proof of purchase (order ID or receipt)
- The return request must be submitted within 30 days of delivery

Items that do not meet these conditions may be rejected or subject to a restocking fee.

---

## Non-Returnable Items

Certain types of goods cannot be returned, including but not limited to:

- Perishable goods such as pet food and treats
- Customized or personalized items
- Gift cards
- Clearance or final sale items

---

## Return Process

To initiate a return, follow these steps:

1. Contact our customer support team with your order details
2. Receive a return authorization and shipping instructions
3. Pack the item securely and include all original materials
4. Ship the item back using the provided return label

We recommend using a trackable shipping service.

---

## Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If approved:

- Refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days
- The refund will be applied to your original payment method

Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

---

## Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged.

If you need to exchange an item:

- Contact support with photos of the issue
- Provide your order details
- We will ship a replacement once the issue is verified

---

## Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received a refund:

1. Check your bank account again
2. Contact your credit card company
3. Contact your bank

If you still have not received your refund, please contact us.
